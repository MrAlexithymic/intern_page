Recent Projects

KARNAVATI

Nestled in the heart of the neighborhood, this charming small shopping complex offers a delightful shopping experience. The interior architecture combines modern design with warmth, featuring open spaces with high ceilings, sleek flooring, and natural lighting pouring in through large windows. The layout is carefully planned, with wide aisles and inviting storefronts, making it easy to browse at leisure. The exterior architecture reflects a contemporary style, with clean lines, large glass panels, and a welcoming entryway. A mix of neutral tones and vibrant accents creates a warm and inviting atmosphere, while small green spaces add a touch of nature. The complex offers various facilities such as ample parking space, clean restrooms, air conditioning, and comfortable seating areas. There are also convenient elevators and escalators for easy access to all floors. Spanning an area of approximately 10,000 square feet, the shopping complex caters to a variety of retail needs, from boutiques to cafes, making it the perfect place for a quick shopping trip or a relaxing break.
